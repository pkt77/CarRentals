FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)



Q: What are your renter requirements for renting in the US?

Ans: Meet renting locations minimum age requirement.
Have a valid drivers license.
Be able to provide an acceptable form of payment.

Q: What Forms of payment are accepted for renting?

Ans: We accept cash, VISA®, MasterCard®, American Express®.

Q: Does Revature Car Rental accept gift cards?

Ans: We dont accept gift cards. We only accept cash, VISA®, MasterCard®, American Express®.

Q: How do I cancel my reservation?

Ans: After confirming reservation if you want to cancel reservation, you have to contact us directly via phone call or email. See contact information section for additional information.

Q: What should I do if the rental car breaks down?

Ans: If your rental car breaks down due to mechanical failure, at first make sure you are safe. Then give us a call for assistance.
